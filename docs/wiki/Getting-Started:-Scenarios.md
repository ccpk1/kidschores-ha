## Overview

Every family is different. These three mock scenarios show creative ways to use KidsChores with badges, rewards, and chore structures that match your family's style.

**What You'll See in Each Scenario**:

- Family profile (ages, interests)
- Badge series they use (with point multipliers)
- Example chores with point values
- Creative reward ideas
- Why this setup works

> [!NOTE]
> **Focus on Badges**: All scenarios emphasize badges as the primary motivation system. While KidsChores includes legacy Achievements and Challenges features, badges provide more powerful and flexible motivation with ongoing multipliers and better tracking. Achievements and Challenges have minimal functionality and will likely be reimagined in future versions.

## Mixing and Matching

**Feel free to combine elements**:

- Use XP language with star badges
- Add seasonal themes to classic family setup
- Create pet care badges in gaming family
- Mix cumulative and periodic badges

**Customization Tips**:

1. **Start Simple**: Pick one badge series (3-4 badges max)
2. **Age-Appropriate Points**: Younger kids need lower point thresholds
3. **Family Values**: Choose themes that match your family's interests
4. **Iterate**: Adjust points and rewards based on what motivates your kids

## About Achievements & Challenges

> [!NOTE]
> **Legacy Features**: KidsChores includes Achievements and Challenges from earlier versions. These have minimal functionality:
>
> - Achievements track single milestones (e.g., "complete 100 chores")
> - Challenges are time-bound goals (e.g., "50 chores in 30 days")
> - Both lack the ongoing motivation and multiplier benefits of badges
> - These features will likely be reimagined in future versions
>
> **Current Recommendation**: Focus on badges for your primary motivation system. Badges provide:
>
> - âœ… Ongoing multipliers (make every chore worth more)
> - âœ… Maintenance requirements (encourage consistency)
> - âœ… Multiple badge types (cumulative + periodic)
> - âœ… Better tracking and visualization
> - âœ… More flexible configuration

## What is KidsChores?

KidsChores is a comprehensive Home Assistant integration that transforms household chore management into an engaging, automated experience for the whole family. Whether you want to assign tasks, track progress, reward achievements, or automate approvals, KidsChores provides the tools to create your perfect system.

**Built for flexibility**: Start simple with basic chores and points, or build complex systems with badges, seasonal themes, and automation. It works for families with toddlers, teens, or adultsâ€”anyone who needs chore tracking.

**Local and private**: All data stays on your Home Assistant instance. No cloud required, no external dependencies, complete control over your family's information.
